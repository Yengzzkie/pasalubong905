OUR STORY

*

Pasalubong 905 is a charming, family-owned establishment that embodies the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines. Located 210 Bond St W Unit A, Oshawa, ON L1J 2L7, this cozy eatery warmly welcomes patrons to experience an authentic taste of Filipino cuisine infused with the warmth of traditional hospitality.

Founded and managed by a passionate Filipino family, Pasalubong 905 prides itself on offering a diverse menu filled with beloved Filipino dishes that evoke nostalgia and comfort.

What sets Pasalubong 905 apart is not just its delicious fare, but also the sense of community and family it fosters. The ambiance reflects a homely atmosphere where guests are treated not merely as customers but as honored guests sharing in the flavors of Filipino culture.
